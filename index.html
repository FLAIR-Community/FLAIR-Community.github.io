Research Areas

Federated Learning

FLAIR lab focuses on Federated Learning (FL), conducting in-depth research on several key areas:

Data Heterogeneity: We study how to effectively train models when data distributions vary significantly across different participants. This includes developing robust optimization algorithms and adaptive model update methods.

Communication Overhead: To reduce the significant communication overhead in FL, we propose efficient compression techniques. We also explore methods to decrease communication frequency and data transmission while maintaining model performance.

Large Language Models + FL: We explore integrating large language models (LLMs) with FL to leverage their powerful capabilities in distributed data environments. This involves effectively training LLMs within FL, optimizing parameter synchronization, and developing FL algorithms suited for LLMs.
